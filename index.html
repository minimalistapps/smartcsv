    Smart CSV

    Discover Smart CSV, your go-to app for effortlessly managing CSV files. With advanced features like intuitive search, visual filters, SQL querying, PDF conversion, and more, it revolutionizes data interaction. Experience efficiency like never before.

    Features¶

    • Your AI assistant – Utilizing the power of AI to effortlessly query and extract insights from your data.
    • SQL query – Turbocharge analytics with the familiar SQL language for lightning fast insights.
    • Search content – Easily to find your content with search & filter feature.
    • PDF perfection – Unlimited rows, personalized colors for beautifully detailed tables in PDFs.
    • Chart your way – Explore diverse charts without limits on chart types and endless data points.
    • Unlimited customization – Explore endless possibilities such as hiding, freezing, aligning columns, and more.
